Enhancing climate resilience of coastal communities through the development of a global ocean-climate seamless forecasting system

First Institute of Oceanography (FIO), Ministry of Natural Resources, China (
Academic institution
)

    Description
    Description

    This commitment aims to establish a global seamless forecasting system to enhance the resilience of coastal communities against climate-related hazards such as storm surges, sea level rise, tropical cyclones, and extreme precipitation. Through large-scale ocean observations, integration of multi-source data, advanced forecasting models, and international collaboration, we are dedicated to providing high-quality seamless prediction products to support disaster prevention, planning, and sustainable development for coastal regions.

    To achieve this goal, we will implement the following measures:

    First, we will deploy a large-scale network of low-cost, intelligent Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) buoys, independently developed by the First Institute of Oceanography, Ministry of Natural Resources of China. This initiative will establish an independent and globally distributed ocean observation network, enhancing the coverage and timeliness of marine data.

    Second, we will adopt advanced artificial intelligence and data assimilation technologies to develop multi-scale seamless forecasting systems. These systems will integrate oceanic, atmospheric, terrestrial, and socio-economic data to provide comprehensive decision-making support.

    Additionally, we will build climate risk management tools tailored to coastal areas, enabling customized forecasts and risk assessments. This will facilitate collaboration among policymakers, scientists, and the public, ultimately strengthening the climate resilience of coastal communities.

    Furthermore, we will establish an open data platform to promote global sharing of climate and oceanographic data. We will work closely with governments, research institutions, non-governmental organizations, and the private sector to jointly improve the forecasting system.

    To ensure the long-term effectiveness of the seamless forecasting system, we will set key performance indicators and regularly evaluate the system’s effectiveness and societal impact. A dynamic feedback mechanism will be implemented to continuously optimize forecasting services. In addition, we will conduct specialized technical training programs for coastal nations and communities to enhance local forecasting capabilities and emergency response preparedness.

    This commitment will establish a global cooperation mechanism to ensure the efficient operation of the forecasting system. We will regularly disclose forecasting results, technological advancements, and policy recommendations to the international community to foster global collaboration. Moreover, we will actively seek multilateral funding support and leverage resources from international organizations, government funds, and private sector investments to ensure the sustainable development of the seamless forecasting system.

    Through concrete actions, this commitment aims to advance the development of a global seamless forecasting system, working together with global partners to enhance the climate resilience of coastal communities and contribute to global climate governance.
