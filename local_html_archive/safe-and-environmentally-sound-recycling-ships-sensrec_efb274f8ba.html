Safe and environmentally sound recycling of ships (SENSREC)

International Maritime Organization (IMO) (
United Nations / Multilateral body
)
#OceanAction57769
  • Description
  • SDGs & Targets
  • SDG 14 targets covered
  • Deliverables & timeline
  • Resources mobilized
  • Progress reports
    Description
    Description

    Objective: Enhance the capacity of participating countries and their ship recycling industry to promote a just transition and put in place sound and effective measures that improve the standards of health, safety, social protection and environmental compliance at ship recycling yards, as required by the Hong Kong Convention, as well as the capacity for environmentally sound waste management of the hazardous and other materials that are generated from the process of ship breaking.

    This action includes interventions at four distinctive levels:
    1. Legal, policy and institutional review in developing countries and SIDS.
    2. Capacity building and training of all stakeholders on ship recycling and hazardous waste management.
    3. Enhanced capacity for the sound management of hazardous waste.
    4. Investment in infrastructure and capacity of private sector to implement best practices.
