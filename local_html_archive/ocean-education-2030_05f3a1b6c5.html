    Description
    Description

    To address the global marine ecological crisis and promote the achievement of SDG 14, this initiative aims to shape young people into the core force of marine protection through three strategies: Knowledge Reconstruction, Emotional Activation, and Action Transformation. Specific objectives include: By 2025, promoting 90% of schools nationwide to incorporate marine protection into their interdisciplinary curriculum systems; stimulating emotional resonance among 5 million young people through gamified learning and art programs; establishing practical bases of the "Coastline Laboratory" to mobilize young people to participate in actions such as beach cleaning and biological monitoring, and forming an advocacy network through the #MyOceanPromise social media challenge. The implementation methods cover the development of VR documentaries and data visualization toolkits to reveal the marine crisis, collaborating with scientists to design the "Blue GDP" curriculum, and setting up educational centers for resource sharing. The ultimate goal is, through the global action network driven by young people, to curb the degradation of the marine ecosystem and inject lasting impetus into the full realization of SDG 14 by 2030.
