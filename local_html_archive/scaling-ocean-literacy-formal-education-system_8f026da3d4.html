Scaling ocean literacy in the formal education system

Office for Climate Education (
Non-governmental organization (NGO)
)
#OceanAction57853

  • SDG 14 targets covered
  • Deliverables & timeline

  • Progress reports
    Description
    Description

    The Office for Climate Education, is collaborating with French CNES and their project SWOT to adapt the latest scientific satellite data on oceans into quality pedagogical climate change resources for teachers. These newly developed activities will be adapted, translated and disseminated in France, Latin America and Africa through OCE's network and flagship projects of ALEC and ARRICE. It is expected that over the next 2 years 5 000 teachers and 300 000 students will directly benefit from integrating ocean literacy into their practices and communities.
