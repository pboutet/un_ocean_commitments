The Human Dimension of SDG 14: Strengthening Human Rights for Sustainable Oceans

BlueRights, COST Action CA23103 “Life, Liberty and Health: Ensuring Universal Protection of Human Rights at Sea” (

    Description
    Description

    BlueRights commits to advance ocean literacy through the organization of training events for stakeholders, students, and academics. These training events will draw attention to the link between the protection of human rights and ocean conservation; highlight that the meaningful implementation of SDG 14 is only possible if the social dimension of sustainability is duly taken into account; raise awareness that the successful implementation of SDG 14 requires it to be effectively linked with other SDGs: SDG 3 (good health and well-being), SDG 5 (gender equality), SDG 8 (decent work and economic growth), SDG 10 (reduced inequalities), SDG 12 (responsible consumption and production), SDG 16 (peace, justice, and strong institutions), and SDG 17 (partnerships for the goals); and provide participants with the tools to advance progress in this area, thus bridging the gap between oceans conservation and protection of human rights.

    Partners

    COST Action CA23103 "BlueRights" is a network of academics, practitioners and stakeholders committed to the strengthening of the protection of people at sea.
